Your Internet History [Molotov Cocktail Recipe]







1. Take a bottle of hundred-proof vodka.

2. Drink half the bottle, offer half to a friend.

3. Fill a third of the bottle with a highly flammable liquid.

4. Grab a thin rag, sock, cloth, etc and tie a knot in it.

5. Splash a bit of the flammable liquid on the rag.

6. Stuff the knot into the neck of the bottle.

7. Light the rag.

8. Throw the bottle as hard as you can.

9. Run far and fast.
